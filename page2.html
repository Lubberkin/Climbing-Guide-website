Sport climbing

There is quite a lot of sport climbing in Prague. All of the following recommendations are large gyms, with both bouldering and sport climbing availabe, but here I focus mainly on the sport climbing part. for bouldering recommendations click here. Here are my top 4 favorite sport climbing gyms:.

BigWall

BigWall is a gym that focuses mainly on sport climbing, although there is a small boulder area I do not recommend going there for bouldering only. Longest routes in Prague, at least 50m rope required. There is also a bar with some food and drinks. Showers available.

Location: Ocelářská 891/16, 190 00 Praha 9 (couple parking spaces availabe, close to Ceskomoravska metro station)

BigWall website

SmichOFF

Smichoff is probably the most popular gym in Prague and it's good for beginners. Which means it's often very full. I recommend going there only outside of peak hours. Otherwise you might have to wait in line, and wil overall have a poor experience. Sport climbing isn't bad there, there are a lot of different profiles available (large boulder area as well). 40m rope will suffice. Bar with food and drinks available. Showers available.

Location: Křížová 6, 150 00 Praha 5-Smíchov (big parking lot, near Smichovske Nadrazi metro station)

SmichOFF website
Trinactka (13)

Further away from the center compared to other gyms, but has both sport climbing and boulder. Sport climbing area is smaller than Smichoff's for example but it's still good. 40m rope will suffice. Part of a bigger sport complex, which includes a restaurant as well. Large parking area. Can get quite busy lately but opening hours are quite good. Using their website you can charge money onto a card, so you don't need to wait in line. Best bouldering out of the 3 options. Two stories of boulders on interesting profiles. Showers available.

Location: Jeremiášova 2581/2, 155 00 Praha 13-Stodůlky (big parking lot, between Luka and Luziny metro stations)

Trinactka website
Jungle Letnany

This is a pretty new gym and it's really big. It has both bouldering and sport climbing The sport routes are pretty short compared to other gyms, but they are very nice and there's a lot of interesting profiles. There is also a wall with competition boulders, which are very difficult, but fun to try. In the future there will also be a kilterboard. 40m rope is enough. Restaurant available. Showers available.

Location: Veselská 699, 199 00 Praha 18 (lot of parking space, reachable by bus)
