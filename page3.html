Bouldering

Since bouldering requires less space, there are more bouldering gyms then sport climbing ones. I have mentioned 3 big gyms which have both here. But here I want to focus on the local bouldering only gyms in Prague. Because in my opinion they are nicer, less busy and smaller, which makes them more cozy. Here are my top 4 bouldering gyms in Prague:

Ultraant

Ultraant a.k.a. Tynska is probably my favorite gym. It's a small local gym in the center of Prague built a long time ago in a cellar. It's been recently renovated and the owners and staff there is very nice. You can tell, that they are also climbers, which is probably why the gym is so good. Classic style of setting (colored papers). Only downside is it's size is a bit annoying during peak hours, because there isn't much space. The opening hours are pretty short so check before going. Bar and showers available. Cards not accepted, so make sure you have cash.

Location: Týnská 622/17 Praha 1, Staré Město (near Namesti republiky metro station)

Ultraant website

Lokalblok

Very similair to Ultraant in most things, but it's slightly larger. Built above a good restaurant of the same name. Showers available. Cards not accepted, so make sure you have cash.

Location: Náměstí 14. října 2173/10, Praha 5, Smíchov (near Andel metro station)

Lokalblok website
BoulderBar

Small gym with interesting boulder and profiles. The highlight is definetly that there's a moonboard here. Bar and showers available. The staff is usually quite cranky and the opening times are quite odd. Check before going.

Location: U Výstaviště 230/11, 170 00 Praha 7-Holešovice (near Nadrazi Holesovice metro station)

BoulderBar website
Hudy Karlin

Hudy is a brand new bouldering gym. Bouldering there is good, but right now the holds are pretty new so skin goes away quickly. It's also quite expensive and is the only gym with the price going higher the longer you stay there (first 3 hours no change, after that you pay per extra hour). They also have a weird way of rating boulders: Each color is a different range of difficulity, which basically means that one difficulity is limited to one set of holds.(only other place that has this is SmichOFF). Bar and showers available.

Location: Křižíkova 91, 186 00 Praha 8-Karlín (near Krizikova metro station)
